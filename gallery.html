2017 Repovesi

My first and toughest hike, but also the one with the best rewards. Stunning views troughout the park. And finally crossing the 55 meter long and 12 meter high hanging bridge. This bridge collapsed the following year.

2 / 5
2018 Liesjärvi

On the first day we rented some fatebikes and found plenty of cantharellus. The second day we had loads of rain but we anway had a blast. The joy of good rain gear!

3 / 5
2019 Teijo

We did many smaller routes. There are many beautiful lakes and a nice climb to the top to see it all from above. Stunning sunset and misty morning.

4 / 5
2020 Isojärvi

Loads of rain had fallen prior our trip and beavers are the main inhabitants here. Several parts were flooded because of that, luckily there was a second campfire place. It was a good mushroom year.

5 / 5
2021 Lapakisto

This is a small park with a lot of variety, there was even a tiny watterfal. It was a very windy and chilly weekend, but with good clothes and sleeping bags we survived easily. I even took a morning swim in.
